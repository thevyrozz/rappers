Pop Smoke, born Bashar Barakah Jackson on July 20, 1999, in Brooklyn, New York, quickly rose to prominence in the hip-hop scene with his distinctive voice and unique sound, blending elements of drill music with hip-hop and trap. His breakout single, "Welcome to the Party," released in 2019

Lil Scoom was an emerging artist in the hip-hop scene, known for his unique sound and captivating lyrical style. Hailing from a vibrant urban background, he blends elements of trap, drill, and melodic rap, creating a sound that resonates with a diverse audience. His music often reflects his personal experiences and the realities of life in his community, making it relatable to his listeners..

YoungBoy Never Broke Again, often referred to as YB, is a prominent figure in contemporary hip-hop known for his raw and emotional style. Emerging from Baton Rouge, Louisiana, he quickly gained recognition with his mixtapes and viral tracks. His music often reflects the struggles and realities of his life, touching on themes of hardship, resilience, and personal growth. Hits like "Outside Today" and "Self Control" showcase his ability to blend melodic hooks with introspective lyrics, drawing listeners into his world..

JayDaYoungan was an influential rapper hailing from Bogalusa, Louisiana, known for his distinct blend of melodic flows and gritty storytelling. He rose to prominence with tracks like "23 Island" and "El P" that showcased his ability to convey raw emotion and reflect on his life experiences..
